Process

From Registration
to Automation in Minutes

A streamlined onboarding flow that intelligently places every new client into the optimal workflow group, instantly.

01

Client Registers

Store owners sign up with their business info and Meta credentials. Our system validates and queues them instantly.

02

Auto Group Placement

The platform auto-assigns each client to a workflow group of 10–15 clients, balancing load across Make.com workflows and Meta Apps.

03

Automation Runs

AI-powered messaging flows activate automatically. Clients receive smart notifications. You manage everything from one dashboard.

Architecture

Smart Group
Distribution

Each workflow handles exactly 10–15 clients and one dedicated Meta App. This prevents API rate limits, distributes compute load, and maximizes message delivery reliability.

10–15 clients per Make.com workflow

Prevents workflow overloading and keeps execution times predictable and fast.

Dedicated Meta App per group

Each group gets its own Meta App instance, keeping messaging quotas healthy and isolated.

Auto-create new groups when full

When a group hits 15 clients, the next registration automatically spawns a fresh workflow and App.
